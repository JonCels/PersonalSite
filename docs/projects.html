About Me
Chess Connect
Chess Connect was a group project done for my engineering capstone. The project took place from September 2022 until April 2023, spanning the last two semesters of my undergraduate degree.

The Chess Connect project is a custom-made chess set that tracks the movement of the pieces as the players play. The Chess Connect set includes a custom wooden board, 3D printed pieces, a built-in touchscreen LCD, hidden embedded components, and the chess connect software. The board has 64 squares, each with an inset LED and a Hall effect sensor underneath.
